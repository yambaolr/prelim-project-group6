Our Story: CENTRO Coffee

A peaceful space for coffee, creation, and community since November 16, 2024.

The Café Vibe

CENTRO Coffee is a small, cozy café located inside Bale Herencia, an old Spanish-style house near Holy Angel University in Angeles City, Pampanga. We offer a peaceful spot where people can think, study, relax, and talk, making us a favorite among students, artists, and local workers.

Business Details

  • Owners: Karen Ong Mercado and Glenn Mercado.
  • Established: Soft opened on November 16, 2024 (running for almost 1 year).
  • Operating Hours: Monday to Sunday, 10:00 AM – 10:00 PM.
  • Location: Inside Bale Herencia, Angeles City, Pampanga.

Customer and Performance Insights

We value customer feedback through a manual suggestion box and interactive Instagram polls. Our marketing focuses on community—offering student discounts, running promo days, collaborating with local food vloggers, and hosting acoustic nights/poetry events.

Business Performance Rating (8/10): We rate our performance an 8/10. The shop continues to attract students and regular customers, and influencers voluntarily promote us, which significantly increases our visibility without major costs.
